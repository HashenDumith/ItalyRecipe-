Drinks - Strawberry Bellini


A Strawberry Bellini is a delightful and refreshing cocktail made with prosecco (Italian sparkling wine) and fresh strawberries. It's a variation of the classic Bellini cocktail, which traditionally consists of peach puree and prosecco. To make a Strawberry Bellini, ripe strawberries are blended into a smooth puree and then added to a champagne flute or glass. The glass is then filled with chilled prosecco, creating a beautiful, bubbly drink with a vibrant pink hue and a sweet, fruity flavor. It's often served as a celebratory or brunch cocktail, perfect for enjoying on a warm day or toasting to special occasions.

ingredients

3 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced

¼ cup confectioners' sugar

1 tablespoon brandy

1 ½ cups chilled sparkling wine

3 large whole strawberries

instructions

step 1

Blend hulled strawberries, confectioners' sugar, and brandy in a blender until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.

step 2

Divide the strawberry mixture among three champagne flutes. Pour 1/2 cup sparkling wine into each flute and stir to combine. Garnish each glass with a whole strawberry.
